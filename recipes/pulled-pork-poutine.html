Pulled Pork Poutine Recipe

Delicious BBQ Pulled Pork Poutine ready to be consumed

This pulled pork recipe is a delight for the entire family. If you're looking for some comfort food, this dish is exactly what you need. While simple, it does take a while to cook, but that makes it perfect for a cozy weekend!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pound pork roast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt and peppers
  • 7 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
  • 4 russet potatoes cut into fries
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese curds
  • 1 cup diced pickles
  • 1 sheet of aluminum foil
  • 1 slow cooker liner

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium high.
  2. Season the pork generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large frying pan and cook the pork on all sides until browned (around 4 minutes each side).
  4. Add the pork to a slow cooker along with the water and BBQ sauce. Cook for 4 hours on high.
  5. Shred the pork with 2 forks.
  6. Place the potato wedges in the centre of a 2 foot long piece of aluminum foil.
  7. Drizzle the wedges with the remaining olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Then fold the foil to make a packet.
  8. Place the packet on the grill and cook for 25-30 minutes until browned
  9. Serve the pulled pork over the potato wedges with cheese curds and pickles
