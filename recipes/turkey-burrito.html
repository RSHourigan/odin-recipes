Turkey Burrito

Delicious Mexican-style burritos

Coming home late? While these burritos are quick and easy to make, that doesn't make them any less delectable!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 7.75 ounce cans hot tomato sauce
  • 1 15.25 ounce can of corn (drained)
  • 1/2 small onion (diced)
  • 1 16 ounce can refried beans
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • 1 16 ounce container sour cream
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Brown the ground turkey in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, corn, and onions.
  3. Lower heat and simmer until liquids are reduced (about 20 minutes)
  4. Fill tortillas with the mixture and add the cheese, beans, and sour cream. Enjoy!
